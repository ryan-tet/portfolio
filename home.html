PROJECT HIGHLIGHTS

TRANSONIC BUSINESS JET PERFORMANCE

What? Perform a comprehensive CFD analysis of a super-midsize jet at Mach 0.95 and 40,000 feet.

How? Symmetric model imported into Pointwise; T-rex grid generated to resolve leading edges. Simulation ran in Fluent to obtain performance characteristics.

GLADOS CUBESAT

What? Create a 3U CubeSat capable of cataloging Resident Space Objects in low earth orbit.

How? Created custom brackets and assembly drawings in CATIA/SolidWorks. Used ANSYS to verify structural load conditions during launch.

RD-180 ROCKET ENGINE NOZZLE PERFORMANCE

What? Design an efficient axisymmetric grid of a rocket at Mach 4 and 100,000 feet for ascent and descent.

How? Approximated nozzle curve in Pointwise. Iterative runs in Fluent using grid adaptation to resolve shock boundaries.
